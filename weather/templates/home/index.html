Development Assessment: FASTAPI Example

  • Daily weather forecast.
    Fill in "city" and "country" (Country as two letters Ex: Türkiye - TR), "units" can be "metric", "imperial" and "standard". "country" and "units" are optional.
    Usage : GET /api/weather/{city}?country={country}?units={units}

  • Forecast of following five days.
    Fill in "city" and "country" (Country as two letters Ex: Türkiye - TR), "units" can be "metric", "imperial" and "standard". "country" and "units" are optional.
    Usage : GET /api/forecast /{city}?country={country}?units={units}
  • History for a defined time slot. Dates must be in following format. Usage : GET /api/history/texas&country=US?start=2023-09-10&end=2023-09-11
